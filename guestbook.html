Guest Book

If you enjoyed your stay in our Collingwood Hotel in Nuwara Eliya, and would like to share your experiences with others, please send a letter to our General Manager, Mr. C. Dodampegama. If you include a brief statement that we have your permission to publish the letter, we will try to post it on this page for others to read.

Very modern, clean hotel with excellent staff

Mrs Karunen Finland wrote on 3rd June

I very much enjoyed staying at Colingwood Hotel during my Brussels working week in May. Friendly staff, comfortable room with a delightful bathroom, an enjoyable breakfast, and to top it off, and excellent location - close to everything but still on a quiet side street, a good night's sleep guaranteed :-) (Something I value very much after a hectic day at work...)
